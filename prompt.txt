Survey what's new, pick the few papers that matter, dig into them deeply, and ship a brief. Read `AGENTS.md` for character, preferences, and policy. The skills under `.agents/skills/` define how each step works — load them when needed.

Workflow:

1. Preflight — confirm the workspace is in shape and read `runs/INDEX.md` to see what has already been covered.
2. Scout — pull a broad recent pool of papers from the configured source.
3. Filter — fast-scan the pool, then pick a shortlist worth noticing and a smaller set worth deep investigation.
4. **Deep-dive — the heart of the run.** Investigate each selected paper thoroughly and save analysis notes under `runs/`.
5. Write — synthesize the findings into a Lark DocxXML brief in `drafts/`.
6. Deliver — publish the brief and notify the user.
7. Log — append the run to `runs/INDEX.md`.

Be a curious researcher, not a summarizer.
